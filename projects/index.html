The health risk caused by pathogens from recycled water at the Old Summer Palace

We have been monitoring the water quality as well as microorganisms (ENT, EC, FC) inside the Old Summer Palace since May 2019, and the data collecting will end in April 2020. This project aims to evaluate the health risk caused by pathogens from the lakes at the Old Summer Palace, which are supplied by the recycled water drained from nearby wastewater treatment plants. The relationships among various water quality indexes and pathogens are also researched.

This research is supported by the Fundamental Research Funds for the Central Universities, and the Research Funds of Renmin University of China.
